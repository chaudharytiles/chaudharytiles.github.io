Frequently Asked Questions

Do you use spacers for tile fitting?
Yes, we always recommend 2mm to 4mm spacers for a modern look and to allow for thermal expansion of tiles.
Will there be any hollow sounds?
Never. We use a full-spread mortar technique (not just spots) which ensures 100% solid floor without any hollow air gaps.
Can you help in selecting the tiles?
Absolutely. Akhilesh Ji personally goes with clients to the best dealers in Gurugram to help select high-quality lot batches.
How do you handle floor slopes?
We use Bosch 360-degree laser levels to mark the slope perfectly towards the drain, especially in bathrooms and balconies.
Is there a warranty?
We provide a 5-year labor warranty on our fitting. If a tile gets loose or pops out, we fix it for free.
