FREQUENTLY ASKED

What brands do you use for waterproofing?
We use Dr. Fixit, Sika, and Fosroc industrial-grade chemicals depending on the site requirements and customer preference.
Do you provide service outside Gurugram?
While our primary focus is Gurugram (DLF, Sohna Road, Golf Course), we take premium projects across Delhi NCR for large villas.
Why is Epoxy Grout better than White Cement?
Epoxy grout is non-porous, meaning it won't absorb water or dirt. It prevents fungal growth and doesn't crack like cement.
