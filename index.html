Frequently Asked Questions

What types of stones do you specialize in?
We specialize in Italian Marble, Granite, Quartz, and all types of premium GVT/PGVT Tiles. Our expertise lies in precision cutting and seamless joints.
Do you provide labor-only services or material too?
We primarily provide expert craftsmanship (labor) under the personal supervision of Mr. Akhilesh Chaudhary. We also assist in material selection from trusted vendors.
What is "Zero-Error" craftsmanship?
It means we use laser-leveling tools and spacers to ensure 100% straight lines, zero hollow sounds, and perfectly matched marble grains.
